The Best Movies according to me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Swadesh

This is the best movie of Shahrukh Khan based on a nasa scientist who came to his village after a very long time and then never goes back.

Mirzapur

This is the crime thriller of a don named Kallen Bhaiya and his son Munna Tripathi, you will enjoy watching this movie

Jailor

Jailor is the latest movie of Superstar Rajnikant Who was a jailor a long time ago but his duty is in his blood and this keeps him kicking.
